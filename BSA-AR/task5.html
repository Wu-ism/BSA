Setup Instructions

Once you connect a player to power, a display, and the internet, you should see a purple activation screen. If you don't and instead see the white on-device setup screen, the player could be behind a firewall (can't connect to the internet) or the OS could be out of date.

You can update the OS in several ways, but one of the easiest methods is to visit our downloads page, save the unzipped files directly to the root folder of an SD card, insert the SD card into the player, and press the reset button. The player will reboot several times and then display the purple activation screen.

If the player is behind a firewall, you will need to work with your network administrator to build a custom setup package or use the players on the local network without BSN.cloud connectivity.
