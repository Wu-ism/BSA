Submitting a Support Ticket

At BrightSign, the primary method of receiving free support is by submitting a support ticket online. If you need immediate support, BrightSign offers paid priority callback support service as well.

To resolve your problem quickly, please provide the following information in order of importance. If you're using a third-party CMS system like Appspace, Signagelive, or Carousel, please check with their support first. If you have contacted their support and they referred you back to us, please indicate that in the description. If this request is a follow-up to an existing support ticket, please indicate that as well.

  • A detailed description: Provide as much relevant information about the issue and the steps you've already taken. Make sure the issue is isolated to the player, ruling out things like bad cables or incorrect signal flow.
  • Player Model
  • Serial Number
  • OS Version
  • BrightSign Network or BSN.cloud network/account name
  • Player logs
  • Zipped presentation files: Navigate to the presentation and press the download button to export the presentation to your computer.
  • Display issues: Manufacturer and model of the connected display.
  • Storage issues: Manufacturer and model of the storage device being used.
