Factory Reset

Another common troubleshooting step is to restore the player to the same settings it had coming from the factory. You can perform a factory reset manually or remotely. To start from scratch: reformat the SD card, disconnect power, then apply power while holding down both buttons, wait for the error light to flash rapidly, release the buttons, and then wait for the device to set itself up. It will reboot itself multiple times.

You can also perform this process remotely by formatting storage and resetting the player.
