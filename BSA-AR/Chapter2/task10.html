The Dealer Training section of our website has all the information you need to get up to speed on the BrightSign player ecosystem. Visit the training page at www.brightsign.biz/training-1/dealer-training-new.

Under Dealer Resources, resellers have access to data sheets, videos, marketing presentations, and sample presentations. Check out the resources at www.brightsign.biz/training-1/dealer-training-new#dealerResources.

New sales associates can enroll in BrightSign Academy at any time to receive additional BrightSign training and RU credits. Email bsa@brightsign.biz for an invitation.

Resellers can use the product selector to help customers choose the best player for their needs. Visit the product selector at www.brightsign.biz/digital-signage-products/product-selector.
