Free Content Cloud and Paid BSN.cloud Subscriptions

Details

  • A Control Cloud subscription is included for free with every player running BrightSign OS 8.0 or above.
  • Control Cloud allows customers to manage and control player networks remotely with a limited feature set.
  • Customers can upgrade to a fully featured cloud-based CMS, called BSN.cloud, for $99/player/year or use a partner's application.
  • Each partner application will vary in services, features, and cost. As an Authorized Reseller, it's helpful to familiarize yourself with our partners so you can help your customers find a software that fits their specific needs.
