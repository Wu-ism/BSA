Key Points for BrightSign Resellers

BSN.Cloud is BrightSign's secure and scalable cloud infrastructure that connects players to a suite of integrated services and software. As a reseller, understanding BSN.Cloud is crucial for advising customers on how to effectively manage their digital signage networks.

  • Two Service Levels: BSN.Cloud offers two main services: bsn.Control (free) and bsn.Content (paid subscription).
  • Integration with Partner CMS: It supports seamless integration with various Content Management Systems, providing flexibility to meet different customer needs.

bsn.Control Service (Free)

Every BrightSign player comes with the free bsn.Control service, allowing basic remote management capabilities.

  • Streamlined Player Setup: Easy activation and initial configuration.
  • Real-Time Monitoring: Access to player health status and diagnostics.
  • Choice of CMS: Customers can select their preferred CMS for content management.

bsn.Content Service (Paid Subscription)

For advanced features, customers can upgrade to the bsn.Content service at $99/player/year.

  • Complete Content Management: Cloud-based hosting, distribution, and scheduling of content.
  • Advanced Network Control: Tools for grouping players, tagging, analytics, and reporting.
  • Smart Access Management: Customizable roles and permissions for team collaboration.

Encourage customers with complex needs or larger networks to consider bsn.Content for enhanced control and efficiency.

BrightAuthor:connected

BrightAuthor:connected is a powerful authoring software that complements BSN.Cloud services.

  • Cross-Platform Availability: Available for PC, Mac, and Chrome.
  • Intuitive Design Tools: Allows creation of engaging digital signage presentations.
  • Secure Connection: Enables real-time monitoring and remote management of players.
