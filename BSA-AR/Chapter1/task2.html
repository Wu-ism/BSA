BrightSign has implemented a Minimum Advertised Price (MAP) Policy to protect the investments of our resellers and maintain the integrity of our brand. This policy requires all resellers to advertise BrightSign products at or above 100% of the list prices as listed on BrightSignâ€™s official web store.

Key Points of the MAP Policy:

  • The policy applies to all resellers advertising BrightSign products online, including websites, email campaigns, third-party platforms, and printed media.
  • Resellers must not advertise prices lower than the official list prices. Current pricing can be found on the MAP policy page.
  • While the MAP Policy governs advertised prices, resellers are free to set their actual resale prices. This means you can sell products at any price, but advertised prices must comply with the MAP Policy.
  • If a reseller violates the MAP Policy, BrightSign may take actions such as canceling orders, restricting future orders, or suspending the reseller's account.
  • Resellers are required to inform any third-party internet sellers of the MAP Policy and notify BrightSign of their contact information.
  • All questions regarding the MAP Policy should be directed to legal@brightsign.biz.

BrightSign reserves the right to monitor advertised prices and enforce this policy unilaterally. The MAP Policy is subject to change or cancellation at BrightSign's sole discretion.

For detailed information, please review the full MAP Policy on our website.
