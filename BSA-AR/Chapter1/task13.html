Training

BrightSign offers you and your customers a wide range of resources.

BrightSign Academy is a new and evolving learning management system focused on the BrightSign player ecosystem. If you are reading this, you are already enrolled in BrightSign Academy. If you would like to continue learning about BrightSign-specific topics, you can email training@brightsign.biz.

We also offer Tutorial Videos that provide step-by-step training on player setup, BrightAuthor:connected, BSN.cloud, and more. The videos build on one another and are composed of short, manageable modules. Follow along or skip to a specific topic. Sample assets are provided.

Visit our Quick Start Guide if you are setting up a player for the first time.

BrightSign Live! is a free training series hosted on Vimeo as a live stream twice a month. These hour-long training sessions take you through various topics related to BrightSign products and services. Register in advance or join us live on Vimeo.

BrightAuthor Custom ($250 per hour) is a paid live online session focused on topics requested by the customer. Topics can relate to any BrightSign product or service. To schedule training or ask a question, email sales@brightsign.biz.

Our Dealer Resources page has even more information and resources to get up to speed on BrightSign.

Additionally, we offer how-to and user guides on our Zendesk site, and more technical documentation is available at our BrightSign Documentation site.
