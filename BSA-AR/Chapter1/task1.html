In this chapter, we'll cover player pricing, our Minimum Advertised Price (MAP) policy, assessing customer needs, the basics of player setup and content publishing, your obligations as a reseller, ordering products, and training resources.

Following this chapter is a section on BrightSign support and a brief quiz. Upon completion of this course, you'll become a BrightSign Authorized Reseller.

Please send the certificate of completion to your distributor as proof of your Authorized Reseller status.
