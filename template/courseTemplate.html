Chapter Overview

In this chapter, you will learn about the basics of setting up your BrightSign player and get a step-by-step walkthrough on getting started. By the end of this chapter, you will have your player ready for content.

  1. Unboxing and Hardware Overview:
    • Identify key components of the BrightSign player.
    • Understand the different ports and accessories included.
  2. Physical Setup of the Player:
    • Connecting the player to a screen, power source, and network.
  3. Initial Configuration:
    • Navigating the on-screen setup wizard.
    • Selecting language and region settings.
    • Configuring network settings (Ethernet or Wi-Fi).
  4. Content Upload:
    • Uploading content (images, videos) via USB or SD card.
    • Previewing the content on the display.
  5. Finalizing Setup:
    • Ensuring the player is connected and ready to play content.
    • Troubleshooting basic setup issues (if any).
